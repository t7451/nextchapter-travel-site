## Usage Notes

### Template Variables
Replace these placeholders when using the templates:
- `{{ short_url }}` - Your affiliate tracking link (e.g., onestack.com/af/yourname)
- `{{ coupon_code }}` - Your personal discount code
- `{{ coupon_percent }}` - Discount percentage (typically 15-25%)
- `{{ affiliate_landing_url }}` - Link to affiliate program signup

### Best Practices
1. **Twitter/X**: Keep under 280 characters. Use short URLs. Tag @1CommerceLLC when appropriate.
2. **Instagram**: Include 3-5 relevant hashtags. Use carousel posts for desk setups.
3. **LinkedIn**: Professional tone. Focus on productivity and quality. Use 3-4 hashtags max.
4. **Timing**: Post during work hours (9am-5pm PT) for best engagement.
5. **Images**: Pair with high-quality photos showing products in use, not just product shots.

### Content Mix Recommendations
- 60% product-in-use (desk setups, carry shots)
- 25% lifestyle/workspace content
- 15% direct product features/reviews

## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: #000000 (Black)
- Secondary: #FFFFFF (White)
- Accent: #333333 (Charcoal)
- Highlight: #00AA00 (Green for earnings/success)

### Typography
- Headers: Sans-serif, bold (e.g., Helvetica Bold, Inter Bold)
- Body: Sans-serif, regular (e.g., Helvetica, Inter)
- Accent: Monospace for codes/numbers (e.g., Courier New)

### Image Guidelines
- High-resolution product photography
- Authentic lifestyle shots (not stock photos)
- 60/40 product-to-lifestyle ratio
- Consistent lighting and color grading

### Presentation Tips
1. Keep text minimal—slides should support your talk, not replace it
2. Use high-quality images that show products in real use
3. Practice transitioning between slides smoothly
4. Have backup stats ready for Q&A
5. Customize examples based on the affiliate's niche
6. Time check: Aim for 15-20 minutes total with Q&A

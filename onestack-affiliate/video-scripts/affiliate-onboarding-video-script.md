# ONE STACK Affiliate Program - Onboarding Video Script

**Duration**: 2-3 minutes  
**Format**: Loom / YouTube (unlisted)  
**Presenter**: Keith from 1Commerce LLC  
**Location**: Office desk with ONE STACK products visible

---

## Pre-Production Notes
- **Camera**: Webcam or DSLR (1080p minimum)
- **Lighting**: Natural or ring light, face well-lit
- **Audio**: Clear microphone (lav or USB mic)
- **Screen Share**: Have dashboard ready to demo
- **Props**: ONE STACK products on desk (tote, bottle, wallet, charger, desk mat)
- **Editing**: Minimal cuts, add captions/text overlays for key points

---

## Script

### HOOK (0:00 - 0:15)

**[Text overlay: "Your First Commission in 3 Steps"]**

---

### SECTION 1: What ONE STACK Is (0:15 - 0:45)
**[Camera pulls back slightly to show desk setup with all products]**

> "So first, what is ONE STACK? It's our merch line for builders."

**[Pick up and show each item as you mention it]**

> "We've got a canvas tote, a 24-ounce stainless steel bottle, a leather wallet, a wireless charger, and a desk mat. These aren't conference giveaways—this is stuff you actually use every day if you write code, run a store, or build products."

**[Text overlay: "Premium gear for daily use"]**

### SECTION 2: What's In It For You (0:45 - 1:30)
**[Screen share: Show commission tier graphic]**

> "Alright, let's talk about what you earn. We pay 20 to 30 percent on every sale you send our way."

**[Point to tiers on screen]**

> "You start at 20% as a new affiliate. Hit $500 in sales in a month, and you jump to 25%. Keep going—$2,000 gets you to 28%, and our top partners at $5,000 a month earn 30%."

**[Switch to calculator or example]**

> "Here's what that looks like in real numbers: If you send 10 sales at an average of $180, that's $360 to $540 in your pocket. 25 sales? You're at $900 to $1,350. And if you're pushing 50 sales a month, you're earning over $2,500."

**[Return to camera]**

> "Plus, top partners get free kits, early access to new drops, and custom partnership deals."

**[Text overlay: "20-30% commission • Free kits • Custom deals"]**

---

### SECTION 3: How to Start (1:30 - 2:30)

> "Okay, how do you actually get started? Three steps."

**[Step 1 - Show dashboard login]**

> "Step one: Log in to your dashboard. You'll find your main affiliate link and your personal discount code right here."

**[Click to copy link, show action]**

> "Copy that link. That's what you'll share everywhere—Instagram bio, YouTube descriptions, Twitter profile, wherever."

**[Step 2 - Navigate to asset library]**

> "Step two: Download the asset pack. Click here to access the library."

**[Show thumbnails of photos, videos]**

> "You'll get product photos, lifestyle images, B-roll clips, and even caption templates. Everything you need so you don't have to create from scratch."

**[Step 3 - Return to camera]**

> "Step three: Post your first piece of content this week. It can be a desk setup photo, an unboxing, or a simple story showing how you use the tote or bottle."

**[Gesture to desk items]**

> "Put your affiliate link in your bio or the video description, drop your discount code in the caption, and you're live."

**[Text overlay: "1. Copy link • 2. Download assets • 3. Post this week"]**

---

### SECTION 4: Best Practice Tips (2:30 - 3:00)
**[Return to camera, lean in slightly]**

> "Quick tips from what we've seen work best."

**[Tip 1]**

> "First: Show YOUR setup. Don't just repost our product shots. People want to see how you actually use this stuff in your daily workflow."

**[Tip 2]**

> "Second: Keep it simple. One clear call to action. Don't overwhelm people with a wall of text—just say 'Use code X for Y% off' and link in bio."

**[Tip 3]**

> "Third: Repost everywhere. Take that one desk photo and turn it into a YouTube Short, an Instagram Reel, a TikTok, and a Twitter post. Maximize your reach."

**[Text overlay: "Show YOUR setup • One CTA • Repost everywhere"]**

**[Lean back slightly, casual close]**

> "I'm excited to see what you build. Let's get your first sale this week."

**Text on screen**:
- Email: keith@1commercesolutions.shop

## Script Notes for Presenter

### Tone
- Conversational, not salesy
- Confident but approachable
- Fast-paced but clear (no rush)
- Enthusiastic about the products

### Body Language
- Make eye contact with camera
- Use hand gestures when showing products
- Lean in for key points
- Smile authentically, especially at close

### Pacing
- Speak at ~140-160 words per minute
- Pause briefly after each main point
- Don't rush through numbers/stats
- Leave space for text overlays to be read

### If Recording Multiple Takes
- Keep energy consistent across takes
- Match desk setup in each take (continuity)
- Re-do any sections where you stumble
- It's okay to read from notes off-camera

### Technical Tips
- Do a test recording first to check audio/video
- Close other apps to avoid notification sounds
- Mute phone and Slack
- Record in 5-10 minute segments, edit together
- Keep raw footage as backup

## Success Metrics

Track these after publishing:
- View count (target: 80%+ of affiliates watch)
- Average watch time (target: 70%+ completion rate)
- Click-through rate on dashboard link
- Time from video view to first post (target: <7 days)
- Affiliate feedback/questions (should decrease over time)

Update video if:
- Dashboard UI changes significantly
- Commission structure is updated
- Common questions arise that should be addressed
- Watch time drops below 60%

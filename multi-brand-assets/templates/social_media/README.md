# Social Media Templates

This directory contains templates for social media posts across various platforms.

## Platforms Supported

- Twitter / X
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- Facebook
- TikTok
- YouTube (descriptions and community posts)

## Template Types

### Post Copy Templates
Ready-to-use caption templates with variable placeholders for customization.

### Image Templates
- Quote cards
- Promotional graphics
- Product showcases
- Announcement templates

### Video Scripts
- Short-form video scripts (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Long-form content outlines
- Live stream structures

## Variables

Common variables used in templates:

- `{{ brand_name }}` - Brand or company name
- `{{ product_name }}` - Product being promoted
- `{{ url }}` - Link to content
- `{{ cta }}` - Call to action
- `{{ hashtags }}` - Relevant hashtags
- `{{ discount_code }}` - Promotional code

## Best Practices

### Character Limits
- Twitter/X: 280 characters
- Instagram caption: 2,200 characters (but first 125 shown without "more")
- LinkedIn: 3,000 characters (but first 140 shown without "see more")
- Facebook: 63,206 characters (but ~100 optimal)

### Hashtag Strategy
- Instagram: 5-10 relevant hashtags
- LinkedIn: 3-5 professional hashtags
- Twitter: 1-2 hashtags maximum

### Visual Guidelines
- Instagram: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- Twitter: 1200x675px (landscape)
- LinkedIn: 1200x627px (landscape)
- Stories (IG/FB): 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)

## Usage

1. Select the appropriate template for your platform
2. Replace variables with your content
3. Adjust tone to match your brand voice
4. Add relevant hashtags
5. Schedule or post immediately

## Tone and Voice

Each template includes notes on:
- Recommended tone (professional, casual, playful, etc.)
- Target audience
- Best posting times
- Engagement strategies
